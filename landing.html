Core features

🧠

Auto-detect

Recognizes the indicator type instantly — IP, domain, URL, MD5, SHA1, SHA256, email or CVE. No dropdown, no manual selection.

⚡

Defang-aware

Handles defanged input and restores usable pivots automatically. Paste hxxps:// or evil[.]ru directly from reports.

🎯

Category filters

Filter platforms by Reputation, OSINT, or Threat Intel. Open only what matters for the current investigation step.

🔗

Shareable links

Use ?ioc= links to share a pivot-ready IOC instantly. Your team lands with the same context in one click.

🔒

Zero leak design

Runs client-side with no built-in telemetry. IOCs never leave your browser until you explicitly open a platform tab.

🔬

Works with Defang IOC

Move directly from extraction to pivoting in one workflow. Use Defang IOC to extract, then pivot each indicator here.
